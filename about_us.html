What We Do?

We provide structural concrete form work engineering and form work design services for all kinds of big and small scale construction projects through out the India. Our proficiency in Formwork solution includes the engineering of proprietary systems, custom designed systems and job built systems. Specific applications that we expert in include the design of formwork for monolithic construction, stair cores, elevator cores, walls, one sided walls, slabs, beams, girders, and other structures. We have expertise with all the key form work manubicturers through out the world.

Complicated, state-of-the art structures, extreme heights and loads are welcomed by our Form work consultants to assist you with the most cost effective and safest way of forming concrete with excellent quality finishes. Form work engineers team with the client’s project team, even at their bidding stage, for evaluating possible formwork solutions and effective planning. Our expertise includes form work engineering and form work design services for buildings, parking floors,podiums, and other concrete structures. We ensure your form work project is handled in a very professional and timely manner with our extensive expertise within the construction engineering industry.

Our team of form work engineers also offer redesign of used Aluminium formwork for your new projects. From our past experience we are able to utilize 70% of inventories as it is and only 15% required modification. Adding these, we are successful in utilizing 85% of inventories and only 15% is bought new that also in few cases. We have completed successfully many projects in this manner and clients are happy because it create a exponential value for their unutilized inventories with our partnership.

Vaba, a leading manubicturer of aluminum formwork systems, was started by Bansal and Sons. The company is currently headed by Chairman Tony Bansal, and the board of directors includes Tony's son, Vansh Bansal, and his daughter, Tanisha Bansal, who is an interior designer.

Tony Bansal - Chairman

Tony Bansal is the current chairman of Vaba and has been instrumental in the growth and success of the company. With over 30 years of experience in the construction industry, Tony has a deep understanding of the needs and challenges of builders and contractors. Under his leadership, Vaba has become a leading manubicturer of aluminum formwork systems and has expanded its product offerings to meet the needs of its customers.

Vansh Bansal - Director

Vansh Bansal is a director at Vaba and has been actively involved in the growth and development of the company. With a background in engineering and business management, Vansh has played a key role in the development of new products and the expansion of Vaba's customer base. His technical expertise and business acumen have helped the company to stay at the forefront of the construction industry.

Tanisha Bansal - Interior Designer

Tanisha Bansal is an interior designer and a member of the board of directors at Vaba. Her expertise in interior design has helped the company to develop innovative and aesthetically pleasing solutions for its customers. Tanisha's designs have been used in various projects, including offices, homes, and commercial spaces. Her contributions have helped to enhance the reputation of Vaba as a company that not only provides quality products but also focuses on the aesthetic aspects of construction.
