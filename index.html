ABOUT US

What We Do?

We provide structural concrete formwork engineering and formwork design services for all kinds of big and small-scale construction projects throughout India. Our proficiency in formwork solutions includes the engineering of proprietary systems, custom-designed systems, and job-built systems. Specific applications that we specialize in include the design of formwork for monolithic construction, stair cores, elevator cores, walls, one-sided walls, slabs, beams, girders, and other structures. We have expertise with all the key formwork manufacturers throughout the world.

Complicated, state-of-the-art structures, extreme heights, and loads are welcomed by our formwork consultants to assist you with the most cost-effective and safest way of forming concrete with excellent quality finishes. Formwork engineers team with the client’s project team, even at their bidding stage, for evaluating possible formwork solutions and effective planning.

Our expertise includes formwork engineering and formwork design services for buildings, parking floors, podiums, and other concrete structures. We ensure your formwork project is handled in a very professional and timely manner with our extensive expertise within the construction engineering industry.

Our Product

VABA Aluminium Formwork

VABA is a leading manufacturers of aluminium formwork systems in India. The company's products are used in a wide variety of construction projects, including high-rise buildings, bridges and tunnels. VABA's aluminium formwork systems offer a number of advantages over traditional steel formwork systems, incluing:

  • Lighter weight, which makes them easier to handle and transport.
  • More durable, with a longer lifespan.
  • More recyclable, which is better for the envirement.
  • More versatile, as they can be used for a wider range of applications.

Features and Benefits

VABA's aluminium formwork systems are made from high-quality aluminium alloy 6061-T6, which is known for its stregth, durability, and corrosion resistance. The systems are also designed to be lightweight and easy to assemble, making them ideal for use in tall buildings and other projects with tight deadlines.

In addition to their lightweight construction, VABA's aluminium formwork systems also offer a number of other benefits, including:

  • High strength-to-weight ratio
  • Excellent dimensional stability
  • Smooth surface finish
  • Easy to clean and maintain
  • Recyclable
