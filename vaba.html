VABA is a leading manufacturers of aluminium formwork systems in India. The company's products are used in a wide variety of construction projects, including high-rise buildings, bridges and tunnels. VABA's aluminium formwork systems offer a number of advantages over traditional steel formwork systems, incluing:

  • Lighter weight, which makes them easier to handle and transport.
  • More durable, with a longer lifespan.
  • More recyclable, which is better for the envirement.
  • More versatile, as they can be used for a wider range of applications.

Features and Benefits

VABA's aluminium formwork systems are made from high-quality aluminium alloy 6061-T6, which is known for its stregth, durability, and corrosion resistance. The systems are also designed to be lightweight and easy to assemble, making them ideal for use in tall buildings and other projects with tight deadlines.

In addition to their lightweight construction, VABA's aluminium formwork systems also offer a number of other benefits, including:

  • High strength-to-weight ratio
  • Excellent dimensional stability
  • Smooth surface finish
  • Easy to clean and maintain
  • Recyclable
